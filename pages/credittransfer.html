What is Academic Credit Transfer

Academic Credit Transfer is a term used by universities and colleges for students who want to continue education or experience more quality institutions during their education time. The main reason behind the credit transfer can be a change in employment of student family members, health issues, dissatisfaction with current institution, or discontinued course.

Benefits of Credit Transfer

Access to different programs

Provides mobility across various disciplines like Degree, Diploma, PG Diploma, Certificate programmes.

Part-time / Full-time modes

Facilitates lifelong learning amongst students in full time and part-time modes.

Maintain academic records

Maintains academic records and issues an official transcript, recognized by all UGC approved universities in India.

Add more options

It will allow students to make specific modifications / specializations to their degree programs rather than undergoing the regularly prescribed degree/courses.

Adjust pace.

It will allow students to choose a pace for their studies along with the associated cost.

Finish in your own time

It enables multiple entry-multiple exit scheme for students to complete their degrees as per their time preferences.
